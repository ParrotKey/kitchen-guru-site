Delete your account & data

Last updated: June 22, 2026

You can permanently delete your Kitchen Guru account and personal data at any time. Here's how, and exactly what happens.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Kitchen Guru and go to You / Profile → Settings.
  2. Tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and associated personal data are permanently deleted.

Want a copy first? Use Settings → Export my data before you delete.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email info@parrotkey.us from the address on your account with the subject "Account deletion request." We'll verify it's you and process the deletion. We aim to complete email requests within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in record (email and authentication account).
  • Your profile (display name, profile photo, profile details).
  • Your age-eligibility record.
  • Your content — bulletin posts, direct messages you sent, court photos, and reviews.
  • Your check-in / court-presence records and friend connections.
  • Your push token and notification settings.

What may be retained, and why

  • Safety records. Reports and block records may be retained as needed to keep the community safe and enforce our rules.
  • Legal & financial. Limited records we're required to keep (for example, subscription/transaction records held by the app stores, or information needed to comply with law).
  • Backups. Residual copies may persist in secure backups for a limited time before being overwritten.
  • De-identified data. Aggregate or anonymized data that can no longer identify you may be retained.
Deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted it can't be recovered, and you'll lose your friends, messages, and any Premium access tied to the account. Cancel an active subscription separately in your App Store or Google Play settings to stop future billing.

Questions

Email info@parrotkey.us or see our Privacy Policy for how we handle your information.
