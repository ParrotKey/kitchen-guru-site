Community Guidelines

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Kitchen Guru works because players look out for each other. These guidelines keep the community welcoming, safe, and fun — on the app and on the court. Breaking them can get content removed or your account suspended.

By using Kitchen Guru you agree to these guidelines, our Terms, and our Privacy Policy. Kitchen Guru is for adults 18+.

Be respectful

  • Treat other players the way you'd want to be treated. Friendly trash talk is fine; harassment isn't.
  • No bullying, threats, intimidation, or stalking — in posts, messages, or in person.
  • No hate speech or attacks based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, disability, sex, gender, sexual orientation, age, or any protected characteristic.

Keep it clean and honest

  • No sexually explicit, violent, or graphic content. No nudity.
  • No impersonation or fake profiles. Be who you say you are.
  • No spam, scams, mass solicitation, or unwanted advertising.
  • Post real court photos and honest reviews — not misleading, off-topic, or others' copyrighted material.

Respect privacy & safety

  • Don't share anyone's private information (theirs or your own sensitive details like home address or financial info).
  • Don't use Kitchen Guru to track, surveil, or locate someone without their consent.
  • Don't solicit minors or anyone under 18, and don't bring anyone under 18 onto the platform.

Play fair on the court

  • Show up if you say you will. Be on time, share courts, and respect facility rules.
  • Settle disputes like adults. Good sportsmanship is part of the deal.

Reporting & blocking

If you see something that breaks these rules:

  • Report any post, message, photo, or profile using the report icon. Reports go to our moderation team for review.
  • Block a user from the chat header or their profile to immediately stop them from contacting you.
  • If someone is in danger or a crime is occurring, contact local emergency services first.

How we enforce

Depending on severity and history, we may remove content, issue a warning, restrict features, or suspend or permanently remove an account. Serious violations — threats, exploitation, or illegal activity — can result in an immediate ban and, where appropriate, referral to authorities. We aim to be fair, but our goal is always to protect the community.

Questions

Reach us anytime at info@parrotkey.us or through Support.
